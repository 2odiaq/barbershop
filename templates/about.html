Our Story

Precision Cuts was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional grooming services in a relaxed and welcoming environment. What started as a small one-chair shop has grown into a premier barbershop with a team of skilled barbers dedicated to their craft.

Our founder, Robert Johnson, began his career as a barber over 15 years ago, learning the traditional techniques that form the foundation of our approach. As the barbershop grew, we've combined these classic methods with modern styles to give our clients the best of both worlds.
